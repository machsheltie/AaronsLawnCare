SPECIFICATIONS INCLUDED
================================================================================

TITLE TAGS:
  • Optimal: 50-60 characters
  • Display: 55 chars (desktop), 30-40 (mobile)
  • Impact: HIGH
  • Best Practice: Primary keyword first

META DESCRIPTIONS:
  • Optimal: 150-160 characters
  • Display: 155 chars (desktop), 120 (mobile)
  • Impact: HIGH
  • Best Practice: Include keyword, location, CTA

CANONICAL URLS:
  • Format: Absolute URL (not relative)
  • Pattern: https://aaronslawncare.com/path
  • Impact: HIGH (duplicate content prevention)

ROBOTS META:
  • Default: index, follow, max-image-preview:large, max-snippet:-1, max-video-preview:-1
  • Impact: MEDIUM (crawler control)

GEO TAGS:
  • Region: US-KY (ISO 3166-2)
  • City: Louisville
  • Coordinates: 38.2527;-85.7585 (lat;long)
  • Impact: MEDIUM (local search visibility)

OG IMAGES:
  • Size: 1200 x 630 pixels (1.91:1 aspect)
  • Format: JPG or PNG
  • Max Size: 500 KB

TWITTER IMAGES:
  • Size: 1200 x 675 pixels (16:9 aspect)
  • Format: JPG or PNG
  • Max Size: 500 KB

  const schema = generateServiceSchema('Service Name', 'Description', '/path');

  <SEOHead {...seoConfig} schemaMarkup={schema} />

Blog Post:
  const seoConfig = getArticleSEO('Title', 'Desc', 'slug', '2025-01-14');
